Delete Your HerdWise Account

If you would like to delete your HerdWise account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email us at herdwise.farm@gmail.com from the email address you used to register your HerdWise account.
  2. Include "Delete Account Request" in the subject line.
  3. We will verify your request and delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • Your HerdWise account and login information
  • All farm, animal, and activity data associated with your account

What Data Will Be Kept?

  • No personal data will be retained after deletion, except as required by law (for example, for tax or legal compliance).

How Long Does It Take?

  • Your data will be deleted within 30 days of your request.

Need Help?

If you have any questions, please contact us at herdwise.farm@gmail.com.

HerdWise is committed to protecting your privacy and data. For more information, see our Privacy Policy.
